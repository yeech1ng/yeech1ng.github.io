Project Diary

Week 8:

1. Finalised Topic

As an avid K-Drama watcher, I felt compelled to explore the topic of Korean Dramas, also known as K-Dramas, and answer the question of: “What makes K-Drama popular?”

Through a factors-type data-story, I will explain the popularity of K-Dramas by analysing and showcasing the specific factors that have contributed to its popularity.
